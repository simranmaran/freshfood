Fresh food is essential for a healthy lifestyle. It includes fruits, vegetables, dairy products, meat, and seafood that are not processed or preserved with chemicals. Eating fresh food provides essential nutrients like vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, which help in boosting immunity and maintaining overall well-being. Fresh fruits and vegetables are rich in fiber, which aids digestion and keeps the body energetic. Unlike processed food, fresh food has a natural taste and is free from harmful additives. Choosing fresh food over packaged or frozen items ensures better health and a balanced diet.
